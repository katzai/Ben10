About Ben 10

The Legend Begins

Ben 10 is an American animated series created by Man of Action Studios and produced by Cartoon Network Studios. The story follows Ben Tennyson, a 10-year-old boy who discovers a mysterious alien device called the Omnitrix during his summer vacation with his Grandpa Max and cousin Gwen.

The Omnitrix attaches itself to Ben's wrist and gives him the ability to transform into 10 different alien heroes, each with unique and incredible powers. With these newfound abilities, Ben takes on the responsibility of fighting evil, stopping villains, and saving the world—all while learning valuable lessons about heroism, responsibility, and growing up.

The franchise has expanded into multiple series including Ben 10: Alien Force, Ben 10: Ultimate Alien, Ben 10: Omniverse, and a 2016 reboot. With its compelling characters, exciting action sequences, and imaginative alien designs, Ben 10 has become one of Cartoon Network's most successful and beloved franchises.
